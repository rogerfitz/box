Our Story

The Nice Package started January 2014 in Champaign, IL when Matteo was looking for an easy way to find cool, new things. Other subscription box services were too focused on satisfying a single niche like cosmetics or the starting price was way too expensive. Care packages already exist but they can be so much more than your mom sending you candy. We knew college guys would appreciate a service made for college guys, by college guys so we started The Nice Package.

We really appreciate our customers input and we do our best to discover products from places like Shark Tank, Uncrate, DudeIWantThat.com, and GentsAmongMen.com. If you'd like to suggest a product please use this form to send us a message.
